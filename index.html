זְהִירוּת · Watchfulness
וְהָיָה לִבְּךָ לִשְׁקֹל מַעֲשֶׂיךָ בְּכָל יוֹם
Let your heart weigh your deeds each day, that none stray unnoticed.
After Mesillat Yesharim · Ch. 2
The teaching

Zehirut — watchfulness — is the first rung of Ramchal's ladder. One examines their ways and weighs them, as a merchant reviews his accounts so nothing slips into loss.

In the language of Kabbalah, every act either raises a spark of light or scatters it. To weigh a moment — in thought, in speech, in deed — is the work of tikkun, mending.

The aim is not guilt but clarity. To see the day truly is already to begin the ascent.
